Fall-off-the-bone delicious and full of smoky flavor!

Ingredients

  • 2 racks of pork baby back ribs
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • Your favorite BBQ sauce (about 1 cup)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 300Â°F (150Â°C).
  2. Remove the membrane from the back of the ribs and pat dry.
  3. In a bowl, mix brown sugar, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper.
  4. Rub the spice mix generously over both sides of the ribs.
  5. Wrap the ribs in foil and bake for 2.5 to 3 hours until tender.
  6. Preheat grill to medium heat. Remove ribs from foil and brush with BBQ sauce.
  7. Grill ribs for 5-10 minutes on each side, brushing with more sauce as desired.
  8. Let rest for a few minutes before slicing and serving. Enjoy!

Tips & Tricks

  • For extra flavor, marinate ribs overnight with dry rub.
  • Use wood chips like hickory or apple for smokier BBQ flavor.
